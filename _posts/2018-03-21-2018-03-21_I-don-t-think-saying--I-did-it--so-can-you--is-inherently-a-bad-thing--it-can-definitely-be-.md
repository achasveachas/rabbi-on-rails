I don’t think saying “I did it, so can you” is inherently a bad thing, it can definitely be encouraging to those working hard when they see someone who already went through it and made it.

We just have to be mindful of others’ circumstances and how they compare to our own.

I used to recommend the bootcamp route I took to pretty much everyone who asked me, now I take a more nuanced approach of encouraging people to take into account their financial situation and to decide (for themselves) if this is really the right choice for them.
