<p name="f7b9" id="f7b9" class="graf graf--p graf--leading">I don’t think saying “I did it, so can you” is inherently a bad thing, it can definitely be encouraging to those working hard when they see someone who already went through it and made it.</p>
<p name="bd26" id="bd26" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">We just have to be mindful of others’ circumstances and how they compare to our own.</p>
<p name="eecf" id="eecf" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p graf--trailing">I used to recommend the bootcamp route I took to pretty much everyone who asked me, now I take a more nuanced approach of encouraging people to take into account their financial situation and to decide (for themselves) if this is really the right choice for them.</p>
