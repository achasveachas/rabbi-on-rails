It should be your Twilio phone number. If I remember correctly they give you the option to set one up when you open the account.

You should be able to find it here: [https://www.twilio.com/console/phone-numbers/incoming](https://www.twilio.com/console/phone-numbers/incoming) (you have to be signed in).

If there are no phone numbers on the dashboard you can hit the red plus button to get one.
